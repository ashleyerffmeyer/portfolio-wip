About Me

Mechanical Engineer turned coder.

I'm a detail-oriented, organized, and creative software engineer who enjoys making unique customer experiences and products. I love working in an industry where art and technology converge. I enjoy fast paced roles where I can see the effects of my work immediately and make changes based on feedback and new innovation.

I recently completed a Full Stack Flex Web Development certification from University of Kansas Professional & Continuing Education. I am adept in JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, APIs, and Node.js. I also gained exposure to MySQL, MongoDB, and React. In addition, I have over 9 years of program management and Six Sigma experience, including an MS in Engineering Management.

In past roles, I spearheaded lean efforts to reduce manufacturing cycle time and increase efficiency, resulting in $682K cost savings. I became proficient in translating high level customer vision and requirements to large project teams. I have a passion for continuous improvement, persistent learning, and customer service.

In my free time, I love to eat and run my way through my adopted hometown of Kansas City. I look forward to Restaurant Week and Rock the Parkway half marathon weekend every year! When I am not out exploring KC, I’m hanging out with my black and tan dachshund named Chunk.
